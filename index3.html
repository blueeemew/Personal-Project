Best Munch of the Month

Soto Betawi

Dapur Solo, Puri Indah Mall

Taste: 4/5
Price: 4/5
Service: 5/5
Cleanliness: 5/5

This cuisine served by Dapur Solo is a cuisine that is considered one of the National Food. It is found in all parts of Indonesia and each region has its own type of Soto. Soto Betawi which is served by Dapur Solo, located in West Jakarta, has not fully represented their region. It is lacking emping. However, it did not fail to be the best munch of the month since it is the food that has the best taste compared to other food such as Rawon and Karedok.
